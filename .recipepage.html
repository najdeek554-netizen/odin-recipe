Lasagna

Description

A delicious homemade lasagna with layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Cook beef and add sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
